TGen A TOEIC Automator Prototype



 
 
 
 
 
This page creates questions similar to questions found on various English tests. See TOEIC Part 5 for an example.

The sentences are chosen from the NYTimes API, and the individual words are processed or retrieved from Wordnik API. The logic is basically: Find the rarest word in the sentence. If it is a noun and has no alternative definitions, then it will be the answer. Then 3 random nouns are chosen of similar rarity and length.

Known issues
  • The wrong answers are randomly generated. Sometimes the "wrong" answer makes sense in the sentence.
  • The wrong answers are never plural, which sometimes makes the correct answer quite obvious.
